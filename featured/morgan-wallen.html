Let's Meet

Morgan Wallen

Morgan Cole Wallen was born on May 13, 1993 in Sneedville, Tennessee, USA. He is a popular Singer and Songwriter, specializes in Country, Country Pop, Southern rock and Country Rock Music

Early Life and Background

Morgan grew up in a small town in Tennessee. His father was a Baptist pastor, and his mother was a teacher. From a young age, he showed an interest in music, learning piano and violin as a child. Originally, Morgan wanted to pursue a career in baseball and was offered a scholarship, but a serious elbow injury ended his sports aspirations.
