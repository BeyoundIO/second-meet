Mission Statement

For every dollar that is given to the charity organizations listed on this website, we pledge to give five times that amount with the support of the celebrities we work with. We believe that we all must give back to society, to the community and to the people.

Gladly, we've seen amazing results and a stunning increase in donations to charity organizations, aside other creatives causes. Our ideal is to pay regular visits to most charity locations in the United States, Britain, Canada and Ireland to ensure that these funds are going into the right places.
