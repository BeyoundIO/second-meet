Our Approach

We've been around since 2020 connecting fans to their favorite celebrities, and also introducing them to upcoming stars who are profound in their various fields. Our obligation is to facilitate virtual or in-person meetings which can be in groups or private depending on the type of member. We make sure to ensure safety and compliance during all engagements popular figures and celebrities across the world.

We often approach celebrities to form a partnership, if and when accept our proposal, we onboard them onto our platform and build a profile for them according to their preferences. Prices for memberships are decided by an average depending on the costs of hosting the meeting and paying the celebrities, aside tax obligations and operational costs.

FAQ and Recent Questions

What are Membership cards?

Membership cards are tiers or levels of digital membership ID's that allow you to schedule appointments with our featured celebrities and access other benefits.

What tiers or levels of Membership cards can I purchase?

There are six primary membership cards that we offer on our platform. The first tier is regular, then classic, silver, gold, platinum, and VIP. All these tiers offer a limited range of benefits up until the highest tier which is the VIP. This card has the most benefits.

I don't have enough money to purchase a membership card, what do I do?

Please relay your concern to our team, we can still help you get a membership card even when you are short of cash. We offer discounts for certain special circumstances and we also offer payment plans that allow you to pay us back in 2 to 6 months. Although this may not be applicable for all cards.

I've paid for a membership card, how do I verify my payment and get my card?

If you haven't made payment via crypto or gift card, please send a screenshot of your payment (crypto transaction hash or gift card code) along with your name and email address - our support will reach out within 24 hours and verify your transaction. After that, you will recieve your membership card in your email.

I have more questions, who do i contact?

If you have more questions, or you don't understand something, please refer to our online support page, or use the floating whatsapp button to chat us via whatsapp, you can also send us an email here management@exoverse.icu and we will get back to you.
